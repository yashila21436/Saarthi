Frequently Asked Questions

Based on the research conducted, here are a few answers to your questions!

How can a student look for volunteering opportunities?

A student can look for the opportunities posted by multiple NGOs by browsing the available options in the "Projects" section. Since, majority of the students were dependent on university announcements and word of mouth, we decided to provide a platform that would streamline the process of volunteering.

How can I (student) ensure that my work hours will get added?

Once a student has completed his/her work, they need to make a request for adding the amount of hours that they have worked for on that particular day. This notification is sent to the NGO and then they can approve it. This ensures that the students' work gets recognized without any hassle. 

When can I (student) expect to hear back from the NGO?

Once a student has applied for an opportunity, they can expect to hear back from the NGO in about a week.
