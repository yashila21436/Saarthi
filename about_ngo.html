What is Saarthi?

About Us


Saarthi is a vibrant community platform dedicated to bridging the gap between non-profit organizations and passionate volunteers eager to make a difference. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to work together toward a shared vision of a better world, facilitated by technology that simplifies the search for meaningful volunteer work.

Become a Volunteer


Are you ready to make a difference in your community? Join us as a volunteer and be part of something meaningful. Whether you're passionate about education, environmental conservation, or social justice, we have volunteer opportunities that match your interests and skills. By volunteering with us, you'll not only make a positive impact on the lives of others but also gain valuable experience, build connections, and contribute to a brighter future for all.  Join us today and start your journey of making a difference!


Become a Partner NGO


Are you a non-profit organization striving to create positive change in the world? Join Saarthi, a vibrant community platform dedicated to connecting passionate volunteers with meaningful opportunities to make a difference. By partnering with us, you'll gain access to a diverse pool of dedicated individuals eager to contribute their time and skills to your cause.
